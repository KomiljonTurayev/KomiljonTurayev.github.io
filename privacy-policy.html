# Privacy Policy — ZeroOne AI Hub **Last updated:** April 18, 2026 ## 1. Overview ZeroOne AI Hub ("we", "our", "the app") is an AI-powered social media automation tool that creates and publishes short-form video content. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when our application interacts with the TikTok API. ## 2. Data We Collect We collect only the minimum data necessary to operate: - **TikTok User ID (open_id):** Used solely to publish videos to your TikTok account. - **Access Token:** Stored locally on your device to authenticate API requests. Never shared with third parties. - **Video metadata:** Titles, descriptions, and hashtags generated by our AI and attached to uploaded content. ## 3. Data We Do NOT Collect - We do not collect your TikTok password. - We do not store your personal profile information. - We do not track your TikTok followers, views, or analytics. - We do not sell or share any data with advertisers or third parties. ## 4. How We Use TikTok API Our application uses the TikTok Content Posting API exclusively to: - Upload video content (`video.upload` scope) - Publish videos to your TikTok account (`video.publish` scope) We do not use the API to read your private data, messages, or followers. ## 5. Data Storage All authentication tokens are stored **locally on your device** in a `.env` configuration file. No data is transmitted to our servers. We do not operate any backend servers. ## 6. Data Retention Access tokens are retained locally until you manually delete them or revoke access from TikTok's security settings. ## 7. Third-Party Services Our app integrates with: - **TikTok API** — governed by [TikTok's Privacy Policy](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/page/global/privacy-policy/en) - **Groq AI** — for script generation (no personal data shared) ## 8. Your Rights You may revoke our app's access to your TikTok account at any time via: **TikTok Settings → Security → Manage app permissions → ZeroOne AI Hub → Remove** ## 9. Contact For privacy-related questions: **zerooneaihub@gmail.com** ## 10. Changes We may update this policy. Continued use of the application constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
